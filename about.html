About Oshurn Financial

Our commitment to Christian financial education and stewardship

Our Story

Oshurn Financial was founded on the belief that financial wellness is fundamentally connected to personal values, faith, and family legacy. We recognized a gap in the financial services industry: a platform that combines rigorous financial education with Christian stewardship principles, treating wealth not as an end in itself, but as a tool for blessing families and communities.

From our earliest days, we've been committed to transparency, ethical practices, and genuine care for the families we serve. We don't claim to manage billions of dollars or serve thousands of clients. Instead, we focus on what we do best: providing education, resources, and insurance solutions that help individuals and families make wise financial decisions aligned with their values.

Our Mission

To help individuals and families steward wisely, protect what matters, build financial confidence, and create a lasting legacy through Christian financial education, tools, insurance solutions, and stewardship resources.

Our Vision

A world where families make financial decisions with confidence, clarity, and values-driven purpose. Where financial security and spiritual growth walk hand in hand, and where generational wisdom and prosperity flow from one generation to the next.

Our Core Values

Faith

Biblical principles and Christian values guide every aspect of our work. We believe that financial wisdom flows from understanding that all resources are ultimately gifts from God, to be managed with integrity, generosity, and accountability.

Stewardship

We believe in responsible management of resources with wisdom and intention. Stewardship means making informed decisions, avoiding unnecessary risk, and ensuring that every financial action reflects your values and serves your family's wellbeing.

Protection

True financial security means protecting what matters most: your family, your health, and your legacy. We're committed to helping families understand insurance solutions and risk management as essential components of a comprehensive financial strategy.

Growth

Financial growth isn't just about accumulation—it's about building the knowledge, skills, and confidence to make wise decisions that compound over time. We're passionate about education as the foundation of lasting prosperity.

Legacy

The most valuable inheritance is often wisdom and values, not just money. We help families intentionally plan to pass on financial security, decision-making skills, and a legacy of stewardship to future generations.

Integrity

We operate with complete transparency. We don't make false claims about assets under management, client numbers, or performance. We educate rather than sell, and we prioritize your family's wellbeing over commission-driven decisions.

Our Future Vision

As we look ahead, Oshurn Financial is committed to:

  • Expanding Financial Education: Developing comprehensive courses, certifications, and resources to empower families at every life stage.
  • Building Community: Creating spaces where families can learn together, share experiences, and support one another on their financial journeys.
  • Enhancing Technology: Developing tools and platforms that make financial planning, tracking, and stewardship more accessible and intuitive.
  • Deepening Insurance Partnerships: Continuously partnering with quality insurance providers to offer comprehensive, affordable solutions tailored to Christian families.
  • Promoting Financial Literacy: Working to increase financial awareness and stewardship in churches and communities through education and outreach.
  • Supporting Generational Transfer: Creating specialized resources and guidance for families navigating the complex process of passing on wealth and wisdom.
